Quick Setup

Setting up Parallax is simple. Just add this script to your code. 

              
                <script type=text/javascript>
                  var projectID="project_id";
                  window.sessionStorage.setItem("project_id", projectID);
                  document.addEventListener("DOMContentLoaded", (event) => {
                    var head = document.getElementsByTagName("head").item(0);
                    var recorderScript = document.createElement("script");
                    var injectScript = document.createElement("script");
                    recorderScript.setAttribute("type", "text/javascript");
                    injectScript.setAttribute("type", "text/javascript");
                    recorderScript.setAttribute("src", "recorder_url");
                    injectScript.setAttribute("src", "inject_url");
                    head.appendChild(recorderScript);
                    head.appendChild(injectScript);
                  });
                </script>

After setting up an account, we'll provide you a unique version of this script for your product.

Frequently asked questions.

Have questions? View answers to commonly asked questions or Contact Us.

Why Parallax?

Even with dedicated onboarding flows, products can initially look overly complex and scare customers away. We want to help those users better engage with those products from day one by identifying their sticking points and guiding them to the completion of tasks.

That way, they can understand the value of the product much quicker and are more likely to continue using your product.

What if I already have an onboarding flow?

Parallax doesn't interfere with your existing onboarding flow. In fact, we allow you to augment your existing flow with a more on-demand experience.

How does Parallax compare to other products in the space?

There are plenty of products providing onboarding software and product tours, but most are heavy and have steep learning curves. We get you off the ground in minimal time.

Other products exist to display your guides and videos, but they require your users to switch between platforms. Parallax keeps it all in-app to reduce friction.

I have a dedicated UX team. Do I still need Parallax?

Your UX team does a fantastic job to optimize your users' journeys, but it still takes time to notice problems, build and test new code, and release it to your users.

We let you provide immediate relief to your users, so they aren't in the cold after they submit a ticket or contact your team.
