What your 153 prospects were calling about

Questions your customers have

  • How does your pricing structure work? Which plan is right for their needs? (45% of calls)

  • How can they contribute feature requests and bug reports? How can they keep track of reports? (20% of calls)

Product Feedback

What are your customers saying about your product?

Organizing and sharing segments.

"They've made some recent improvement here but it can still be a little cumbersome to share segments with teammates. And you quickly end up with a ton of different segments "starred" so you don't lose them." — research call

"Not very robust segmentation and has limitations for what filters you can add to segments. No way to pull in data from other sources like a CRM." — CRM data

Better organization in the notes feature.

"I'd love to be able to group my notes together or use tags so that I can see common themes emerge." — sales call

Positive UI feedback.

"The playback interface is intuitive and the ability to automatically skip inactive time saves us a lot of time in reviewing activity." — research call

Negative UI feedback & complaints.

"The UI is quite busy... I'm not what I would consider to be a 'power user' of the tool yet so at times it can seem a bit overwhelming." — sales call

"The images you see are sometimes misaligned just enough to be inaccurate. An example is that a piece of UI may appear not on-screen in FS, but when we look it actually is on-screen. A relatively minor inconvenience, given the rest of the value." — CRM data

"They need something in the UI to show that your matched result falls under which session, and allow you a quick link to the rest of that session. There are times I need to see what happened well before the error happened. A quick link to the rest of that session prior to my error point would be slick." — research call

Pricing

  • Customers are potentially willing to pay more monthly for more support for your product. Potential higher price tier for more planning & counsel?
  • A few pricing concerns, specifically with clarity about pricing structure, but most seem very happy with the value they get. You could potentially charge more, since they seem to primarily value customer service over pricing sensitivity.
